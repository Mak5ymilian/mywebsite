About Me

I was introduced to computers from a young age, where I gained interest and experience in how they work. My family and friends always asked me for help if they had any issues with technology, from basic troubleshooting to significantly more difficult challenges like reinstalling an operating system or replacing faulty hardware. At 12 years old, I built my first computer just from watching YouTube videos and doing research. I continue to build new computers as well as upgrade them or troubleshoot them. I gained more knowledge and experience after building my first PC as I played around with software and hardware daily to today where I completed a degree Cyber Security and work in this field.
I decided to study Cyber Security at university as I believed it was the best career path, considering my passion for security and the increasing number of bad actors and the bigger need for improved security.
