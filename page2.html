Vulnerable System Exploitation and Prevention

Within a small team, we outlined modern security challenges such as the ones in the OWASP top 10 and their causes. We later set up an environment. I identified its vulnerabilities by using OpenVAS and I decided to exploit two vulnerabilities through privelage escalation managing to gain root access. We provided our recommendations to remediate the vulnerabilities and how to secure systems overall.
